About Me

Hello there! My name is Gabriel Moretson, and I am a freelance artist specializing in illustration and concept art. I have a strong passion for bringing unique and compelling characters and worlds to life through my art. I work with clients on a commission basis, and I pride myself on delivering high-quality work that meets their specific needs and vision. Whether you're looking for a character design, a book cover illustration, or anything in between, I'm here to bring your ideas to life. Feel free to contact me to discuss your project and see how we can work together!
